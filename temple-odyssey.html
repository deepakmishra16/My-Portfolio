TEMPLE ODYSSEY

by Team MetaMatrix

TEMPLE ODYSSEY is a virtual reality (VR) game being developed by "TEAM METAMATRIX" that allows users to journey through iconic temples. Through interactive storytelling, character dialogues, and animated sequences, users will be able to witness the historical, legal, and mythological stories surrounding these sacred sites which will help them to understand their culture, it's establishment and why the knowledge of our culture plays a vital role in our overall persona. VR makes it lucid for the upcoming generations to indulge into our rich history.

By offering users an opportunity to virtually visit temples, we aim to provide an emotional and educational experience that fosters cultural understanding, while also preserving the stories and history of these spiritual sites for future generations.

    Unique Selling Points of Temple Odyssey

  • The game not only focuses on the commonly known facts but also delves into untold historical, legal, and mythological stories related to the temples. This unique content offers insights that are rarely covered in mainstream narratives.
  • By utilizing modern technology like VR, the game effectively bridges the generational gap, making it easier for younger generations to connect with ancient traditions.

    Our Future Plans for Temple Odyssey

  • At first, there will only be one temple in the game to explore and know the story of and that will be "Ram Mandir" and then we will add more temples in the game which is not covered in mainstream media with updates.
  • At first, there will only be one character with one POV and then we will add different characters in the game with different POVs such that the player can experience the untold stories of the temple with different perspectives.
